Write or freewrite to a prompt. They can be anything as long as they spark off ideas. You don't necessarily need to stick to the prompt. Once you're writing, you can go off on tangents.

Where to find prompts;

  • Bristol Tonic is a night where poets and musicians chose a prompt and have a month to write something.
  • Write in response to a piece of art.
  • Don't think, just embrace randomness
