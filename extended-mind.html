The Extended mind

This refers to the theory that your mind extends beyond your body, that actually we store memories in physical places as well. These physical places (such as this Tiddlywiki, or a notebook), then become extensions of our thinking.

Animals display this behaviour as well in an example of panpsychism:

Just as octopuses appear to outsource information-processing tasks to their tentacles, or crickets to their tracheal tubes, perhaps spiders outsource information processing to objects outside of their bodies — their webs.

Networked thought perhaps? (Sorry.)
