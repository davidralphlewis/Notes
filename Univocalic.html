Univocalic

A poem which only uses one vowel. So words need to only have one ofa, e, i, o, u and sometimes y. A form of [[constrained writing|Constrained-writing].]

  • Cathy Park Hong's Ballad in A is an excellent example of the form
Marshall’s a marksman, maps Kansan’s track,
calm as a shaman, sharp as a hawk,
