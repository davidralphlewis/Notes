Zettlekasten

Invented by Nicholas Lumen, this is a knowledge management system that has gained popularity in recent years due to How to Take Smart Notes.

It's a series of small notes linked together. Lumen used index cards and created a network of interconnected notes, using an alphanumeric system to get paths and see clusters of thought.

Strictly, this is not a Zettlekasten, it is a wiki. But the principles have helped me to design it. And you need to avoid tool fundamentalism anyway.
