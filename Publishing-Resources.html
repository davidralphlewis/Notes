Places to find publishers

There's a whole world of small presses and publishing houses out there beyond the big few. Here's some other website that are good starting points to find a press that is a good fit for your work.

Poetry

  • Angela T Carr's website a dreaming skin publishes monthly lists of presses that have submission calls.
  • The Poet's directory is a fantastic resource, listing publishers, presses and magazines.
  • Neon magazine have a big list of UK poetry publishers. Selcouth Station have a long list of smaller presses and zines
